### 1. Field Matching (Bulk Upload)

After upload:

| Your Header | Map To | Action |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| `Mobile` | `phone` | Select |
| `Full Name` | `first_name` + `last_name` | Split |
| `Notes` | `custom_field` | Create new |

Click **Preview** → Fix errors → **Import**

---

### 2. Add Individual Contact

1. Go to **Contacts** → **+ Add Contact**
2. Fill:
   - **Phone**: `+15559876543`
   - **Name**: `Sarah Johnson`
   - **Tags**: `vip, dental`
   - **Custom Fields**: `Budget: $50k`, `Source: Referral`
3. Click **Save**

---

### 3. Agency Workflow

| Step | Action |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Add VIP lead in **Master Account** |
| 2 | Tag: `client-xyz-vip` |
| 3 | **Assign to Client Portal** |
| 4 | Client calls lead → AI uses **custom fields** in prompt |
