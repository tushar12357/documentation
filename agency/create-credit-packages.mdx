### 1. Build a Package
- **Agency Dashboard** → **Billing** → **Credit Packages**
- Click **+ New Package**

| Field | Example |
|------|--------|
| Name | `Starter AI Calls` |
| Credits | `1,000` |
| Price | `$97` |
| Billing | Monthly |
| Bonus | `+100 free` |

---

### 2. Client Assignment
- Assign to **specific clients** or **all**
- Auto-renew → Auto-charge
- Client sees: **“Your AI Plan: 1,000 calls/mo”**

---
<Tip>
Offer **tiered plans**: Starter ($97), Pro ($297), Unlimited ($597)
</Tip>
