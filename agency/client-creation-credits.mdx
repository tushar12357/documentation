### Steps
1. **Add Client** (as above)
2. Go to **Client Profile** → **Credits**
3. Click **+ Add Credits**
   - Amount: `500`
   - Note: `Q4 Bonus`
4. Done — Client sees updated balance

---
<Warning>
Manual credits **do not auto-renew**.
</Warning>
