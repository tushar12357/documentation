### 1. Enable Custom Domain
- **Client Settings** → **Domain**
- Enter: `ai.client.com`

---

### 2. DNS Records to Add

| Type | Host | Value |
|------|------|-------|
| CNAME | `ai` | `proxy.closerx.ai` |

> Takes 5–30 min to propagate

---
<Note>
SSL auto-issued. No cert management.
