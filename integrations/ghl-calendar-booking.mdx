AI says: “I’ve booked you with Mike on Tuesday at 3pm” — and it’s **already in GHL**.

### 1. Enable in Agent
1. **Agent Editor** → **Tools** → **GHL Calendar**
2. Select **Event Type**
3. Enable **Auto-Book**

---

### 2. Client Handoff
- Clone agent → Connect to **client’s GHL calendar**
- Assign → Client sees **their bookings**
