### 1. Connect Your GHL Account
1. **CloserX Dashboard** → **Integrations** → **GHL**
2. Click **Connect**
3. Log in to GHL → Authorize
4. Select **Location (Subaccount)**

---

### 2. Two-Way Sync

| Direction | Data |
|---------|------|
| **GHL → CloserX** | New leads, tags, custom fields |
| **CloserX → GHL** | Call outcome, recording link, booked appt, AI summary |

---

### 3. Client-Specific Setup
1. **Clone** integration per client
2. Connect to **client’s GHL subaccount**
3. Assign → Client sees **only their data**
4. Whitelabel: Show **client logo** in call logs

---
<Tip>
Use **GHL tags** like `ai-called`, `appt-booked` to trigger automations.
</Tip>
