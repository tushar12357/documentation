### 1. Link GHL Calendar
1. **Integrations** → **GHL** → **Calendars**
2. Select **Subaccount** → **Calendar** → **Event Type**
3. Map:
   - `Demo Call` → `30-min Consult`

---

### 2. AI Uses GHL Calendar
- Checks **team availability**
- Books into **correct calendar**
- Updates **GHL opportunity**

---
<Note>
Works with **round-robin** and **team calendars**.
</Note>
