### Setup Overview
1. **Campaigns** → **Callbacks** → **+ New Rule**
2. Trigger: Missed call after 10 min
3. Agent: "Follow-Up Bot"
4. Time Window: 9AM–6PM next day

---

### Client Use
- Assign to **client's phone number**
- Logs in **GHL as "Callback Attempt"**
- Whitelabel: "From Your Team"

Supports **voicemail drop** + SMS reminder.
