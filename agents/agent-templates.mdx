Jumpstart your AI agents with our library of ready-made templates tailored to common business scenarios. Customize them to fit your exact needs without starting from zero.

### 1. Access the Template Library
- Navigate to **Agents** → **Create New Agent** → **Use Template**.
- Browse by:
  - **Use Case:** Sales, Support, Booking, Follow-Up
  - **Industry:** Real Estate, Healthcare, SaaS, E-commerce
  - **Goal:** Lead Qualify, Book Demo, Collect Payment

### 2. Preview & Select
| Template | Best For | Key Features |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Real Estate Closer** | Follow-up on leads | Qualifies budget, books viewings |
| **Appointment Booker** | Service businesses | Syncs with GHL/Calendar |
| **Support Bot** | Inbound helpdesk | Transfers to human on escalation |
| **Debt Collector** | Finance | Polite reminders, payment links |

- Click **Preview** to hear a sample call.
- View full prompt, tools, and settings.

### 3. Customize
- Edit prompt to match your brand voice.
- Change LLM, voice, or language.
- Add/remove tools (e.g., SMS follow-up, CRM tag).
- Save as **"My Custom Template"** for reuse.

### 4. Deploy
- Assign to a phone number or campaign.
- Launch immediately or schedule.
