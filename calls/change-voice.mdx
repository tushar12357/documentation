### 1. Access Voice Settings
- Open **Agent Editor** → **Voice & Language**
- Browse by:
  - **Gender**: Male / Female / Neutral
  - **Accent**: US, UK, AU, IN, ES, FR, etc.
  - **Tone**: Warm, Professional, Energetic, Calm
  - **Age**: Young, Adult, Senior

### 2. Preview Voices
| Voice ID | Sample | Best For |
|--------|--------|---------|
| `emma-us` | "Hi, this is Emma from..." | Support, Booking |
| `james-uk` | "Good afternoon, James here..." | High-end B2B |
| `sofia-es` | "Hola, soy Sofia..." | Spanish markets |
| `raj-in` | "Namaste, Raj speaking..." | India outreach |

Click **Play** to hear in real-time.

### 3. Customize Voice
- **Speed**: 0.8x (slow) → 1.5x (fast)
- **Pitch**: -20% to +20%
- **Pauses**: Add natural breaks after questions
- **Emotion Tags**: `happy`, `empathetic`, `urgent`

### 4. Assign to Client (Whitelabel)
1. **Clone** master agent
2. Change voice to match **client’s brand** (e.g., female for spa, male for finance)
3. Save as: `ClientX - Branded Voice`
4. **Deploy to Client Portal** — they hear **their own voice**

---
<Tip>
Use **warm female voices** for healthcare, **confident male** for sales.
</Tip>
