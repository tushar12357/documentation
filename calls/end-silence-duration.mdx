### Settings Explained

| Setting | Default | Recommended |
|-------|--------|------------|
| **End on Silence** | 8 sec | 6–10 sec |
| **Max Call Duration** | 15 min | 5–10 min (sales), 3 min (support) |

---

### How to Configure

1. **Agent Editor** → **Call Settings**
2. Set:
   - **Silence Timeout**: `8` sec
   - **Max Duration**: `10` min
3. Customize **timeout message**:
   > “I think we lost you. I’ll send a follow-up text!”

---

### Client Customization

- **Spa Client**: 5 min max, 6 sec silence
- **Solar Sales**: 12 min max, 10 sec silence
- Clone → tweak → assign

---
<Tip>
Shorter durations = higher **connect rate** in outbound.
</Tip>
