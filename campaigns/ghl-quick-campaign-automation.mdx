### Supported Triggers

| Trigger | Type | Speed |
|---------|------|-------|
| **Pipeline Stage Change** | Native | Instant |
| **New Contact Added** | Native | Instant |
| **Tag Applied** | Native | <10 sec |

---

### Setup: Most Popular

1. Go to **Automations** → **Quick Campaign**
2. Connect **GoHighLevel Agency**
3. Select:
   - Subaccount  
   - Pipeline  
   - Stage (example: `New Lead`)
4. Choose the **AI Campaign** to run
5. Enable **Instant Trigger**

> Lead moves to selected stage → CloserX calls in <60 sec → Books appointment

---

### Client-Specific Routing

1. Create **one Quick Campaign per client**
2. Connect the client’s **GHL subaccount**
3. Client sees **only their own leads**
4. AI uses **their calendar + scripts**
