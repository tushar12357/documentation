---

### Setup: GoHighLevel (Most Popular)

1. **Agency Dashboard** → **Integrations** → **GHL**
2. Connect your **agency GHL account**
3. Select **subaccount** → **pipeline stage** → `New Lead`
4. Map:
   - `Phone` → `phone`
   - `First Name` → `first_name`
   - `Tags` → `tags`
5. Enable **Auto-Sync**

> New leads → Instantly in CloserX → AI calls in <60 sec

---

### Client-Specific Import

1. **Clone** CRM connection per client
2. Connect to **client’s GHL subaccount**
3. Assign → Client sees **only their leads**
4. AI uses **their pipeline stages** in prompts
