### Steps

1. Click **Profile** (top right) → **Settings**
2. Go to **Security** → **Change Password**
3. Enter:
   - Current password
   - New password (8+ chars, 1 number, 1 symbol)
   - Confirm new password
4. Click **Update Password**

> You’ll be logged out — log back in with new password

---
<Warning>
Never share your password. Enable **2FA** in Security tab.
</Warning>
