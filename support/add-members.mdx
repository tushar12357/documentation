### 1. Invite a Member

1. **Settings** → **Team** → **+ Invite Member**
2. Enter:
   - Email: `mike@team.com`
   - Role: `Admin`, `Agent Editor`, `Viewer`, `Client`

| Role | Can Do |
|------|--------|
| **Admin** | Everything |
| **Agent Editor** | Edit agents, run campaigns |
| **Viewer** | See dashboards, no edits |
| **Client** | See only their data |

3. Click **Send Invite**

---

### 2. Client Portal Access

- Assign **Client role** → They log in to **their branded portal**
- See only **their agents, campaigns, analytics**
- Cannot access **agency billing**

---
<Note>
All actions are **logged** in audit trail.
</Note>

---
<Tip>
Use **Viewer role** for stakeholders who need reports only.
</Tip>
